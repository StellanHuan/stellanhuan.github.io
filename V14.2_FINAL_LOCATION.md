## If visual still appears wrong

1. max-width:1400 makes sections wider — may look "stretched"
2. aspect-ratio:4/5 makes about image portrait
3. These are the ONLY changes from V12.4

## To restore exact V12.4 look
Change max-width back to 1100px on .masonry and .duo.
Change .about-visual img back to max-height:600px, remove aspect-ratio.
